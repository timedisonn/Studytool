Tim's Pomodoro Timer

Hi! Welcome to Tim's Pomodoro Timer. The Pomodoro Technique is an attention remedy for perfectionists and procrastinators of all kinds. Work in focused intervals. Take a break. Repeat. You can set the timer and break durations and start, stop, or reset the timer. The default cycle is 25 minutes of work, followed by a 5-minute break. After 4 cycles, there is a longer, 30-minute break. Good luck!
